    Strive for Excellence
    We started Accuracy Accounting 18 years ago with a vision in our mind of bringing exccellence to the field of accounting. We worked around the clock, to build the company from the ground up and introduce a fast, safe, and secure busniess for all of your accounting needs.
    From the Start
    We opened our first location in 2000 in Roberts Wisconsin. At the start, we only had two employees and were working out of a small office building. We mainly did busniess around the small town of Roberts and quickly became the go to accounting firm for anyone around.
    New Locations
    By 2005, we had been expanding drastically and now supported 3 firms housing over 40 employees. Accuracy accounting had taken a boom in busniess due to our amazing customer satisfaction, and our core principles of completing tasks in a fast, safe, and secure manner.
    Awards
    By 2010, Accuracy Accounting had been doing extremely well. We had expanded into Michigan and Minnesota, opening more firms and extending our services to million of people. Towards the end of 2010, we had the honor to be awarded the title of best accounting firm in Wisconsin. This was a monumental achievement and reflected on the great services that we provide at Accuracy Accounting
    Dreams coming true
    By 2015, we had expanded throughout most of the Midwest. Accuracy Accounting had became a staple name in the Accounting industry. We had continually followed our core principles, and this lead us to getting metioned in many top accounting news sites, and get partnerships with top brands such as google and nike.
